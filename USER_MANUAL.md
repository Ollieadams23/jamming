# Total Jamms - User Manual

Welcome to Total Jamms! This guide will help you get started with managing your Spotify playlists.

## ðŸš€ Getting Started

### What You Need
- A Spotify account (free or premium)
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection

### First Time Setup
1. Open the Total Jamms application in your browser
2. Click "Log in with Spotify" 
3. You'll be redirected to Spotify - enter your credentials
4. Click "Agree" to give Total Jamms permission to access your playlists
5. You'll be redirected back to Total Jamms automatically

## ðŸ“± App Layout

Total Jamms has a clean, two-panel layout:

### Left Panel - Your Playlists
- Shows all your existing Spotify playlists
- Displays playlist name and track count
- Click any playlist to edit it
- **"+ New Playlist"** button to create a new playlist

### Right Panel - Music Search & Editing
- **Search Bar**: Find music in Spotify's catalog
- **Search Results**: Shows tracks matching your search
- **Playlist Editor**: Edit the currently selected playlist

## ðŸŽµ How to Use Total Jamms

### Searching for Music
1. Type in the search bar on the right side
2. Results will appear below as you type
3. Each result shows:
   - Track name
   - Artist name
   - Album name
   - **"+"** button to add to your playlist

### Creating a New Playlist
1. Click **"+ New Playlist"** in the left panel
2. The right panel will show an empty playlist
3. Type a name for your playlist in the text field
4. Search for music and add tracks
5. Click **"Save to Spotify"** when you're ready

### Editing Existing Playlists
1. Click on any playlist in the left panel
2. The playlist will load in the right panel
3. You can:
   - Change the playlist name
   - Add new tracks by searching
   - Remove tracks by clicking the **"-"** button
4. Click **"Save to Spotify"** to save your changes

### Adding Tracks to a Playlist
1. Use the search bar to find music
2. Click the **"+"** button next to any track in the search results
3. The track will be added to your current playlist
4. You'll see it appear in the playlist track list

### Removing Tracks from a Playlist
1. In the playlist track list, find the track you want to remove
2. Click the **"-"** button next to the track
3. The track will be removed from the playlist
4. Remember to save your changes!

### Saving Your Changes
1. After making changes to a playlist, click **"Save to Spotify"**
2. A green checkmark (âœ“) will appear when successfully saved
3. Your changes will sync with your Spotify account
4. The left panel will update to show the latest playlist information

### Deleting a Playlist
1. Load the playlist you want to delete
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (appears next to Save for existing playlists)
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted
4. The playlist will be permanently removed from your Spotify account

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
**Problem**: Can't log in or login button doesn't work
**Solutions**:
- Make sure you have a Spotify account
- Try clearing your browser's cache and cookies
- Check that you're using a supported browser
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled

### Playlists Not Loading
**Problem**: Left panel shows "No playlists found"
**Solutions**:
- Make sure you've given the app permission to access your playlists
- Try logging out and logging back in
- Check that you have playlists in your Spotify account

### Search Not Working
**Problem**: Search doesn't return results
**Solutions**:
- Make sure you're connected to the internet
- Try different search terms
- Check that your Spotify account is in good standing

### Changes Not Saving
**Problem**: Playlist changes don't appear in Spotify
**Solutions**:
- Make sure to click "Save to Spotify" after making changes
- Check for the green checkmark (âœ“) confirmation
- Verify you have permission to edit playlists
- Try refreshing your Spotify app

### App Seems Frozen
**Problem**: App becomes unresponsive
**Solutions**:
- Refresh the browser page
- Clear browser cache and reload
- Log out and log back in
- Check browser console for error messages
